Our Package

Bali

Bali is Indonesia's most popular tourist destination, renowned for its beautiful beaches, exotic temples, and rich, unique culture. You can enjoy the beauty of Kuta Beach, explore art and culture in Ubud, visit the majestic Besakih Temple, witness the sunset at Tanah Lot, and relax at the exclusive Nusa Dua Beach.

Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta is known as the center of Javanese culture and art, with many historical sites, temples, and rich cultural heritage. Don't miss the world wonder Borobudur Temple, admire the architecture of Prambanan Temple, explore history at the Yogyakarta Palace (Keraton), shop for souvenirs at Malioboro, and enjoy the beauty of Taman Sari.

Papua

Papua offers extraordinary natural beauty with tropical rainforests, mountains, and well-preserved indigenous cultures. The main attractions include the underwater beauty of Raja Ampat, the cultural richness of the Dani people in the Baliem Valley, the UNESCO World Heritage site of Lorentz National Park, the cultural festival at Lake Sentani, and the challenging climb of Puncak Jaya.

NTT

East Nusa Tenggara is known for its diverse natural beauty, including beaches, small islands, and unique culture. Tourists can meet the Komodo dragons on Komodo Island, enjoy the stunning views in Labuan Bajo, marvel at the colorful Kelimutu Lake, relax on the unique Pink Beach, and explore the cultural and natural beauty of Sumba.

Lombok

Lombok offers beautiful beaches, stunning volcanoes, and a more serene atmosphere compared to Bali. Visitors can hike the majestic Mount Rinjani, enjoy the white sand at Kuta Lombok Beach, swim and snorkel at Gili Trawangan, watch the sunset at Senggigi, and relax at Tanjung Aan Beach.

West Sumatra

West Sumatra boasts breathtaking natural scenery, distinctive Minangkabau culture, and delicious cuisine. Explore the city of Bukittinggi with the iconic Jam Gadang, enjoy the natural beauty of Harau Valley, relax by the serene Lake Maninjau, and savor local culinary delights at Padang Beach.
